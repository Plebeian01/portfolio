About

Bridging rigorous research
with human-centred AI.

I'm a UX researcher and applied AI practitioner with a focus on explainability, trust, and the human experience of machine intelligence. My work sits at the intersection of cognitive psychology, interaction design, and machine learning - asking not just what a model predicts, but how people make sense of those predictions.

I hold an MSc in Applied AI & UX Design from Abertay University (graduated with distinction, 2025) and a BA in UX Architecture from Michigan State University (2023). I'm based in Detroit, MI, and open to remote roles and relocation.
